Pork Adobo (For Filipinos)

Pork Adobo is a iconic Filipino dish featuring pork (belly or shoulder) braised in soy sauce, vinegar, garlic, bay leaves, and peppercorns until tender and glazed.

Ingredients

  • Garlic
  • Onion
  • Pork
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Bay Leaves
  • Peppercorns
  • 7 tbsp soy sauce
  • Sugar
  • Water
  • Vinegar

Method

  • Heat oil in a pan
  • Sauté chopped garlic in heated oil
  • Add chopped onions shortly after
  • After sautéing the garlic and onions, add the pork(any cut)
  • Mix the garlic, onion and pork around the pan
  • Add salt and pepper then mix again
  • Add bay leaves and whole peppercorns then mix
  • Stir pork until cooked
  • Add 6-8 tablespoon if soy sauce
  • Add 1tablespoon of oyster sauce(optional)
  • Stir and let simmer for 2 minutes
  • Add sugar (optional)
  • Add water
  • Cover and boil until soft
  • Add more water if needed
  • Add three tablespoon of vinegar
  • Don't cover or stir
  • Simmer until sauce thickens
  • Test with a fork if soft
  • Once soft and the sauce is thick, serve in a bowl and enjoy!
