Breaking Bad

Breaking Bad is a critically acclaimed television series created by Vince Gilligan. It follows the transformation of Walter White, a high school chemistry teacher, into a powerful drug manufacturer.

Visit Official Website

Plot

After being diagnosed with cancer, Walter White turns to manufacturing methamphetamine to secure his family's financial future. He partners with former student Jesse Pinkman, leading them into the dangerous world of drugs and crime.

Cast & Creative Team

Bryan Cranston as Walter White
Aaron Paul as Jesse Pinkman
Anna Gunn as Skyler White
Dean Norris as Hank Schrader
Created by Vince Gilligan

Main Characters

Walter White – A chemistry teacher turned drug kingpin.
Jesse Pinkman – Walter’s former student and partner.
Skyler White – Walter’s wife.
Hank Schrader – DEA agent and Walter’s brother-in-law.

Reviews

Breaking Bad is widely regarded as one of the greatest TV shows ever made. Critics praise its storytelling, character development, and performances.
